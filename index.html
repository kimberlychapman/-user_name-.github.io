A Few Words About Me

My professional and academic career has been dedicated to developing multimodal programs and tools for culturally/linguistically diverse students, and students with diverse learning needs.

Creative

One of my greatest strengths as a designer is my immense creativity and my ability to create user experiences that are engaging and meaningful for a diverse audience.

Collaborative

I have worked collaboratively with experts in African Studies, Transnational Studies, and Technology and Human Behavior—on numerous projects— to design learning experiences that are culturally relevant, and allow students to explore topics/concepts in a variety of physical and digital spaces.

Knowledgable

In addition to my creativity and ability to design for a diverse audience, what sets me apart as an ed tech designer, is my eight years of experience as a teacher and leader for Chicago Public Schools: as a special education teacher on the West Side and South Side of Chicago, in my Diverse Learner Advocacy and Policy work, and in my role as an instructional consultant.

Portfolio

Tangible Technology Phonics App

A tangible technology phonics web application (for users ages 5-9) that pairs physical game pieces with a device: allowing users to engage in auditory, visual, and tactile learning simultaneously.

Cross-Curricular
Dinosaur Unit

Designed a cross-urricular Dinosaur Unit (for students grades 2-5), which incorporates coding and creative writing/storytelling, and concludes with a Dinosaur Excavation performance task.

Borders Museum Exhibit

Served as the Designer and Co-developer of an interactive museum exhibit that explores the history of the U.S.-Mexico border. The exhibit incorporates inclusive features for individuals with diverse learning/orthopedic needs, and offers an application that allows visitors to have a more personalized exhibit experience.

Integrated Rap

Designed an Integrated Multimedia Rap Curriculum: "Rap as Literacy and History", for high school students, in collaboration with an African Studies PhD student (Northwestern University).
